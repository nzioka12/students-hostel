More details & tips

  • Safety first: ask about neighbors, lighting and security at night.
  • Utilities: check whether water and electricity are included or extra.
  • Study environment: ask if there are quiet hours and study rooms available.
  • Transport: proximity to matatu stages or campus shuttles can save time and cash.
  • Documentation: always get a written receipt and a simple tenancy agreement when paying deposit.
